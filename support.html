Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Distraction Dodge?

Tap only the highlighted target shape when it appears. Ignore all other shapes and distractions on screen. Each correct tap charges your ninja's calm meter; each wrong tap drains it.

What does the Focus Ninja's calm meter do?

The calm meter reflects your accuracy over the session. As you ignore distractions successfully, your stick-figure ninja visibly relaxes ‚Äî from jittery and frantic to still and zen. It's a visual reward for your improving focus.

How long is each round?

Rounds are 30 seconds. Short by design ‚Äî perfect for neurodivergent minds that need immediate feedback without long time commitments. Play one round or chain several together.

Is this based on real science?

Yes. The core mechanic is inspired by cognitive inhibition training, which strengthens the brain's ability to suppress irrelevant stimuli. It's been studied as a helpful technique for improving attention in neurodivergent minds.

Does the app collect any data?

No. Distraction Dodge is 100% local. No accounts, no analytics, no ads, no internet required. All your session data stays on your device.

Distraction Dodge is available as a one-time purchase. No subscriptions, no in-app purchases.
